27 August 2018

Rohesie updated:

  • Synths are now de-whitelisted, just remember to set up your Synthetic name first, David.
  • Big Firearms rebalance:
  • SMGs have their damage buffed, but also have higher falloff.
  • The PPSh fires like an inaccurate and weak machinegun from hell. Not horribly effective, but can kill weak T1.
  • Pistol damage and accuracy buffed.
  • The highpower automag now has the shooting speed and stunning effect of a slug loaded tactical shotgun, but much less accuracy and damage
  • Revolvers ammo and shotgun slugs have now a special effect: stagger and slow. The target won't be able to use special abilities (T1 and T2 only) and will get a tiny slowdown. Both for a small time.
  • Tactical shotgun has less accuracy and damage to avoid making it OP with semi-automatic staggerstun. The HG shotgun got extra accuracy and lower scatter, at the cost of firing speed.
  • Buckshot now has a little less falloff, but a little extra negative AP
  • Flechettes damage and AP buffed, though they still spread.
  • Rifle regular ammo have been given a little extre penetration. Rifle AP ammo had damage lowered but penetration increased. Overall falloff is halved.
  • Bonus pointblank damage reduced a bit to weaken the buckshot meta.
  • softed the wield delay: it will now retain the unwield stats until the delay ends and not simply fail. Added a busy icon for feedback
  • Attachments reworked: Suppressor falloff reduced; Bayonet increases side of the gun; Extended barrel as well, but slighty reduces scatter; Smaller damage reduction for the recoil compensator; Quickfire shooting speed reduction halved; Mag harness doesn't reduce accuracy anymore; Scope increases the burst delay and wield delay; Mini-scope has a smaller burst delay; stock no longer lower the fire rate, but inscrease wield delay; bipod greatly reduces burst spread, doesn't slow the rate of fire and is more intuitive to use but has considerable wield delay and increased weapon size.
  • Extra projectiles now have a scatter chance. Buckshot is still 100% as before, flechettes are 80%.
  • Removed two errors which were preventing attachments from affecting falloff damage.
  • Fixed Marksman ammo having negative AP.
  • Pointblank now works on Queen and crushers.
